Music enters this site indirectly. It rarely becomes the subject of an essay, yet it is present behind many of them: in late-night reading, long walks through Guadalajara, obsessive rewrites, unfinished translations, and the peculiar mood that precedes writing.

The charts below are not a canon of the "best" music I know. They are something more embarrassing and therefore more truthful: a record of recurrence. These are the artists, tracks, and tags I return to when I need company, pressure, or a different kind of silence.
