For the moment this door is closed.

Too many requests reached the site at once, and the rate limit stepped in. If you are a reader, the fix is simple: wait a little and come back. If you are a script, a scraper, or a bot moving too quickly through the archive, slow down.

There is no drama in this. Even an archive needs a threshold.
